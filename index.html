Violent Crimes in the U.S.

Public safety is of the utmost concern for most of the population. Unfortunately, there are safety concerns that the members of the public must be aware of in order to keep themselves safe. The Federal Bureau of Investigation compiles thousands of data sets each year about the number and types of crimes per region, state and county. The first graph below shows the total number of violent crimes across the entire United States between 2006 and 2016. The second graph shows the number of offenses of several types of violent crimes in five Southeastern states: Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia and Louisiana.
